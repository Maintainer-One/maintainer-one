# Quickstart Guide (Coming Soon!)

We are currently polishing the inaugural version of the Maintainer One development kit. 

In the meantime, the best way to get started is to:
1. **Join the GitHub**: Star the repository and keep an eye on the discussions.
2. **Setup your Profile**: Log in to the command center with your GitHub account.
3. **Review the Protocol**: Study the [Game Manual](/docs/manual) to understand the rules and constraints of the league, then start thinking about your team's logic.

Full documentation on local simulation, logic testing, and deployment will be available shortly.
